Our Services

18,000Sq Ft Shop
Island Craftsmanship

Rooted in St. Thomas Since the 1980s

What began as a small woodworking shop has grown into one of the Virgin Islands' premier millwork operations. From our 18,000-square-foot facility on Subbase, our skilled team handles everything in-house — design, fabrication, finishing, and installation.

We've crafted everything from island kitchens and custom furniture to large-scale hospitality and retail interiors throughout the Caribbean. We understand these islands — the salt air, the humidity, the light — and we build for them.

Every project is Caribbean Inspired and Caribbean Made, finished to a standard we're proud to put our name on.
